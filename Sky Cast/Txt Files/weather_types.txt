☀️ Sunny: Clear skies with lots of sunlight.
🌤️ Partly Cloudy: A mix of sun and clouds.
☁️ Cloudy: Sky is mostly covered with clouds.
🌧️ Rainy: Rain falling from clouds.
⛈️ Thunderstorm: Rain with thunder and lightning.
❄️ Snowy: Snowfall due to freezing temperatures.
🌫️ Foggy: Low visibility due to water vapor.
🌪️ Windy: Strong winds, sometimes stormy.
